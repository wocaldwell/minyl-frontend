Hi! Thanks for checking out miNyl!

Is your vinyl record collection getting the best of you? miNyl is a web app for music lovers that helps you manage your analog collection in our digital world. Search for songs in your collection and keep track of all those records you gotta have.

To see a full demonstration of all that miNyl has to offer just click on the 'Demo Login' button in the navbar. From there you'll be able to veiw an example user collection. You can even search for songs and/or add a new record to your miWants want list! Thanks again for stopping by!
